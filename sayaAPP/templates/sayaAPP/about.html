SOBAT RELIEF AGENCY (SRA) formerly known as Sobat Area Youth Association (SAYA) is non-profitable organization formed in June 2016 by a group of Youth Intellectuals in Mandeng to bridge gap in the area of youth and women empowerment, promotion of peaceful activities and conflict resolution in Sobat Area, Upper Nile and South Sudan in general. SRA has been registered under South Sudan Relief and Rehabilitation Commission under chapter 3, section 10 of NGOs Act, 2016 with REG. NO, 4778 on 29th March 2023.
