Empower the youth

SOBAT RELIEF DEVELOPMENT ORGANIZATION(SRDO) strives to aid, develop and empower the community members in the villages, regardless of age or gender, religious beliefs and grant access to live saving humanitarian assistance and developmental projects. We recognize that children learn when their physical, spiritual, emotional, and social needs are met. SOBAT RELIEF AGENCY (SRA) will work with community elders, families and the children themselves, to identify on-going needs in order to improve their lives and increase their hope, faith and to have a Peaceful South Sudan free from Poverty, Environmental degradation, Hunger and Diseases

Service beyond self

SOBAT RELIEF DEVELOPMENT ORGANIZATION(SRDO) acts as integrates self-development and individual concerns with public concerns, focusing on higher, broader, and more public levels of service. The organization conducts its activities for the sake of others, whether the public at large or a particular segment of the public. NGO practitioners are to be exemplars of genuine giving out of concern for the welfare of others without the primary goal of their own enhancement or profit.

ABOUT SRDO

SOBAT RELIEF DEVELOPMENT ORGANIZATION(SRDO) formerly known as Sobat Area Youth Association (SAYA) is non-profitable organization formed in June 2016 by a group of Youth Intellectuals in Mandeng to bridge gap in the area of youth and women empowerment, promotion of peaceful activities and conflict resolution in Sobat Area, Upper Nile and South Sudan in general. SRA has been registered under South Sudan Relief and Rehabilitation Commission under chapter 3, section 10 of NGOs Act, 2016 with REG. NO, 4778 on 29th March 2023. SRA which was formerly registered as SAYA in February 2022 by Upper Nile SSRRC with registration NO, 052 changed its name after an approval of name change by its Board of Directors one year later after it signed a partnership agreement with ADRA-SS
